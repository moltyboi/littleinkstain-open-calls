## Source Evaluation

When adding new sources, consider:
- **Signal density** — How many relevant opportunities vs. noise?
- **Timeliness** — How early are opportunities listed?
- **Reliability** — Are listings accurate and current?
- **Alignment** — Does the source favour aligned themes?
