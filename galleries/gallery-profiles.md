## High Priority — Outsider & Mental Health

### Bethlem Gallery
**Type:** Institutional (hospital-based)
**Location:** Bethlem Royal Hospital, Beckenham, UK
**Focus:** Art by people with lived experience of mental health conditions
**Artists Represented:** Wide range of artists with mental health experience
**Submission Policy:** Open submissions for exhibitions
**URL:** bethlemgallery.com

**Alignment Notes:** Definitive UK venue for mental health art. Historic collection. Very high alignment.

**Relationship Status:** Unknown

---

### Pallant House Gallery (Outside In)
**Type:** Institutional
**Location:** Chichester, UK
**Focus:** Outsider art, self-taught artists
**Artists Represented:** UK outsider artists via Outside In programme
**Submission Policy:** Annual Outside In exhibition open call
**URL:** pallant.org.uk / outsidein.org.uk

**Alignment Notes:** Key institutional supporter of outsider art in UK. Gallery has serious collection.

**Relationship Status:** Unknown

---
